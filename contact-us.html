Help & Guide to Use the Website

  • Semester-wise Notes and Syllabus: Go to the "Syllabus" or "Notes" section. Select your semester and download PDF notes or syllabus.

  • Download Books: Visit the "Books" section to access semester-wise textbooks and reference materials in PDF format.

  • Previous Year Question Papers: Use the "Question Papers" section to find and download past exam papers semester-wise and subject-wise.

  • PDF Notes and Study Materials: All topic-wise notes are under the "Notes" or "Study Material" section. Select semester and subject to download.

  • Important Notices and Updates: Check the "Notice Board" or "Updates" section for latest academic news, exam dates, and changes.

  • How to Search and Download: Use the dropdown menus on each page. After choosing your semester/subject, click "Submit" to view/download PDFs.

  • Contact and Help: For doubts or custom requests, use the "Contact Us" page to send a message or upload files directly.
